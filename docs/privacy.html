Privacy notice

This page explains what information may be collected through the MEYELens website and how it is used.

1. General information

The MEYELens website is an informational website for the MEYELens project. We aim to collect only the data that is necessary to operate the website, respond to messages, and understand general website usage.

2. Contact forms

If you use a contact or interest form on this website, the information you provide may include your name, email address, and the content of your message or request.

This information is used only to review your message, respond if needed, or contact you regarding the specific request you submitted.

The information submitted through these forms is not used for marketing and is not sold to third parties.

3. Analytics and cookies

This website may use Google Analytics to understand general website traffic and improve the site.

Analytics is activated only if you accept it through the cookie banner. If you decline, analytics is not enabled by the website script.

Cookies or similar technologies related to analytics are used only after consent, where applicable.

4. How your data is used

  • To respond to messages sent through the website
  • To review expressions of interest in kits, workshops, or educational formats
  • To understand general website usage if analytics is accepted
  • To maintain and improve the website

5. Data sharing

Information submitted through the website is not shared for advertising purposes and is not sold to third parties.

Data may be processed through technical service providers used to operate the website or store submitted form data.

6. Data retention

Messages and interest submissions are kept only for as long as reasonably necessary to review requests, reply, or manage project-related communications.

7. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you submitted through the website, where applicable.

For privacy-related questions, you can use the contact page of this website.

This notice may be updated over time if the website features or data-processing activities change.
